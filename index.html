Art Nouveau

1890-1910

This movement is characterized by organic, flowing lines and natural forms, emphasizing artistry and craftsmanship.

It emerged as a reaction against the academic art, eclecticism, and historicism of the 19th century. It began around 1890 and reached its peak at the turn of the 20th century. The movement was inspired by natural forms and structures, particularly the curved lines of plants and flowers.
